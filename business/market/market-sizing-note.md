# Byproduct Market Validation & Sizing

## Target Market: M20-M25 Construction Blocks
Our blocks are M20-M25 grade (24 MPa compressive strength, ≥ 90% of OPC concrete). Comparable to fly-ash bricks or AAC blocks.

## Market Sizing Note
If 0.01% of India's eco-block market adopts our blocks:
- **Volume**: 6,000 Cr/yr × 0.0001 = ₹60 lakh/yr in block sales alone
- **Plus CCTS credits**: 4,670 tCO₂e/yr × ₹1,850 = ₹86 lakh/yr
- **Total**: ₹1.46 Cr/yr per 0.01% market share

At 0.1% adoption: ₹14.6 Cr/yr (1,000 ton CO₂e/yr × ₹1,850 + block sales)

**Production capacity needed**: 30-50 ton blocks/day at 0.1% share (3-5 plants)
