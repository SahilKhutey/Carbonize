# Technical Datasheet: Coral-Inspired Biomineralization Capture System (CBMS)

## 1. System Operating Envelopes

The following limits define the operational parameters for retrofitting the CBMS reactor into industrial slipstreams.

| Parameter | Nominal Range | Critical Limit | Metric |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Inlet Gas Temperature** | $40\text{--}45$ | $>50$ (Enzyme deactivates) | $^\circ\text{C}$ |
| **Volumetric Gas Flow Rate** | $1,000\text{--}10,000$ | $12,000$ (Overpressure bypass) | $\text{Nm}^3/\text{hr}$ |
| **Inlet \(\text{CO}_2\) Volume Fraction** | $12.0\text{--}15.0$ | $>20.0$ | $\text{vol}\%$ |
| **Inlet \(\text{SO}_2\) Loading** | $800\text{--}1,200$ | $>2,000$ (Acidifies reactor) | $\text{mg/Nm}^3$ |
| **Inlet \(\text{PM}_{10}/\text{PM}_{2.5}\)** | $30.0\text{--}50.0$ | $>100.0$ (Clogs fine meshes) | $\text{g/Nm}^3$ |
| **Reactor Operating Pressure** | $1.01\text{--}1.05$ | $>1.15$ (Emergency Stack Vent) | $\text{bar}$ |

## 2. Reagent Material Specifications

Specifications for the raw feed material streams to ensure optimal carbonation rates.

### 2.1 Chitosan Polymer Matrix
*   **Source:** Cationic marine shrimp shell biowaste.
*   **Degree of Deacetylation:** $\ge 85.0\%$.
*   **Average Molecular Weight ($M_w$):** $150\text{--}300\text{ kDa}$ (low molecular weight for viscosity suppression).
*   **Water Solubility:** High solubility in weak organic acids (pH $<6.0$).
*   **Ash Content:** $<1.0\%$.

### 2.2 Recombinant Carbonic Anhydrase
*   **Variant:** *CA-KR1* (alkali-stable, sulfur-tolerant).
*   **Turnover Number ($k_{\text{cat}}$):** $\ge 1.0 \times 10^6\text{ s}^{-1}$ at $40^\circ\text{C}$ and pH 8.5.
*   **Michaelis Constant ($K_{\text{M}}$):** $8.5\text{ mM}$.
*   **Immobilization Support:** Titanium mesh structure or magnetic $\text{Fe}_3\text{O}_4$ nanoparticles.

## 3. Cured Composite Block Properties

Performance metrics for alternative-concrete blocks compacted and cured over 48 hours.

*   **Bulk Density:** $1,850\text{--}2,100\text{ kg/m}^3$ (comparable to lightweight concrete).
*   **Compressive Strength (28-day):** $20\text{--}25\text{ MPa}$ (matches Indian Standard IS 2185 Grade A).
*   **Water Absorption Rate:** $<8.0\text{ wt}\%$ over 24-hour total submersion.
*   **Leaching Safety compliance:** Passes EPA Method 1311 (TCLP limits: $\text{Pb} < 5.0\text{ mg/L}$, $\text{Hg} < 0.2\text{ mg/L}$).
*   **Carbon Sequestration Index:** $\approx 150\text{--}180\text{ kg of permanent CO}_2$ solidified per tonne of composite block.
*   **Thermal Conductivity:** $0.65\text{--}0.78\text{ W/(m}\cdot\text{K)}$ (better insulation than conventional red clay bricks).
