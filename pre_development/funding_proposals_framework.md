# Funding Proposal Frameworks

This document contains application frameworks and project abstracts for the three targeted funding pipelines.

---

## 1. DST-NIDHI PRISM (Promotion of Innovation, Startups & MSMEs)
*Target Phase: Phase-I (Innovator Grant for Prototype Development, Up to ₹20 Lakh)*

### 1.1 Project Title
Development and Bench-Scale Validation of a Coral-Inspired Biomineralization Multi-Pollutant Solidification Grid (BMSG) for Clean Industrial Retrofits.

### 1.2 Technology Innovation
The innovation lies in utilizing a cross-linked biopolymer matrix (chitosan) stabilized with recombinant carbonic anhydrase enzymes (*CA-KR1*) to absorb and crystallize carbon dioxide, sulfur dioxide, and heavy metals directly from industrial slipstreams into solid, construction-grade blocks in a single unit operation, bypassing high-temperature liquid regeneration systems.

### 1.3 Budget Breakdown (₹20 Lakh)
*   **Fabrication of 10L Column & Gas Manifold:** ₹5,00,000
*   **Purchasing 300-bar Compaction Press:** ₹4,00,000
*   **Chemicals, Enzymes, Gases & Consumables:** ₹6,00,000
*   **Analytical Testing & Characterization fees:** ₹3,00,000
*   **Travel, Contingency & Admin:** ₹2,00,000

## 2. BIRAC BIG (Biotechnology Ignition Grant)
*Target Phase: Up to ₹30 Lakh for Biotechnology Translation*

### 2.1 Project Title
Biotechnological Optimization and Scaling of Enzyme-Catalyzed Biomineralization Carbon Capture.

### 2.2 Biotechnology Focus
This proposal focuses on the biochemical aspects of the CBMS:
1.  **Enzyme Stabilization:** Covalent immobilization of *CA-KR1* onto magnetic nanoparticles to enable enzyme recovery and reduce operational replacement costs.
2.  **Expression Scaling:** Fermentation scaling of *CA-KR1* using recombinant *E. coli* strains to lower enzyme production costs from laboratory rates (₹40,000/g) to commercial bulk targets.

## 3. DST-CERI (Clean Energy Research Initiative)
*Target Phase: Larger Consortium Grant, Up to ₹50 Lakh*

### 3.1 Project Title
Consortium-Led Development of a 1,000 Nm³/hr Industrial Biomineralization Carbon Capture Slipstream Pilot.

### 3.2 Collaborative Execution
This project will be executed as a consortium between:
*   **Biomimetic Solidification Systems Pvt. Ltd. (Lead Developer):** System design and packaging.
*   **IIT Bombay CCU CoE (Academic Partner):** Analytical verification and process engineering support.
*   **Industrial Partner (Steel/Cement Plant):** Slipstream hookup, pilot utility access (flue gas, power, water), and on-site curing yards.
