# Job Description: Senior Process Engineer (CBMS Project)

## Position Overview
*   **Role:** Senior Process Engineer (FTE 1.0)
*   **Location:** Mumbai / Partner Academic Labs (IIT Bombay)
*   **Reporting to:** Principal Investigator (PI) / Project Board
*   **Timeline:** Pre-Development & Prototype Development Phase (Year 1)
*   **Compensation:** Competitive, aligned with premium research engineering scales in India (IIT/DST grade scale plus startup allowances)

## Core Responsibility
The Senior Process Engineer will lead the chemical process engineering, sizing, and design of the **Coral-Inspired Biomineralization Capture System (CBMS)**. This includes transitioning the system from Tier 0 simulation models to Tier 1 laboratory testing and Tier 2 bench prototype columns.

## Key Deliverables
1.  **System Process Design Package (PDP):** Finalize Process Flow Diagrams (PFDs), Piping and Instrument Diagrams (PIDs), General Arrangement (GA) layouts, and Equipment Datasheets for the 10L bench prototype and 1,000 Nm³/hr slipstream pilot.
2.  **Experimental Rig Sizing:** Design the gas mixing and flow controller manifold to simulate industrial coal/cement flue gas mixtures (CO₂, SO₂, NOₓ, and heavy metals).
3.  **Static Mesh Absorber Tower Design:** Optimize mesh configuration, flow patterns, L/G ratios, and ultrasonic demolding integration.
4.  **Vendor & Procurement Coordination:** Manage vendor interactions for custom column fabrication, hydraulic presses, sensors (NDIR, electrochemical, CEMS), and titanium mesh screens.
5.  **Safety & Interlock Specification:** Program regulatory controls (PLC/DCS schemes) for overpressure bypasses and thermal protection triggers.

## Required Qualifications
*   **Education:** M.Tech / PhD in Chemical Engineering from a premier institution (IITs, IISc, ICT Mumbai).
*   **Experience:** 5+ years of practical engineering experience in process design, pilot plant operations, carbon capture systems, gas-liquid columns, or FGD systems.
*   **Software Skills:** ASPEN Plus / HYSYS, AutoCAD, MATLAB/Python, and CFD package familiarity (OpenFOAM, ANSYS Fluent).
*   **Competencies:** Hands-on lab construction, safety protocols, PID loop calibration, and familiarity with Indian environmental compliance (CPCB norms).
