This document outlines the usage guidelines for CBMS Technologies' trademarks, including names, logos, and product identities.

The following marks are protected trademarks of CBMS Technologies Pvt. Ltd.:

*   **CBMS** (company abbreviation)

## 2. Permitted Use Cases
You may refer to the protected marks without prior written permission under the following conditions:
*   **Academic Citation**: Referring to the CBMS-Sim platform in peer-reviewed journals, white papers, or reports (please use the preferred citation format in `CITATION.cff`).
*   **Educational Context**: Discussing the simulator's architecture in lectures, slides, or academic courses.
*   **Development Reference**: Developing third-party tools or integrations that work with the CBMS-Sim API (e.g., "Plugin for CBMS-Sim").

## 3. Prohibited Use Cases
You may NOT use the protected marks in the following scenarios without a formal written license:
*   **Commercial Branding**: Labeling a commercial carbon capture product, service, or business entity using "CBMS" or "CBMS-Sim".
*   **Visual Assets**: Embedding the official CBMS logo on any marketing website, presentation, or product sheet not published by CBMS Technologies.
*   **Domain Registration**: Registering domain names that include "cbms", "cbms-sim", or similar variants (e.g., `cbms-scrubber.com`).
