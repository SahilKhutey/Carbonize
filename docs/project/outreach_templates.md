# Collaborative Outreach Templates for Lab Partnerships

This document compiles the formal outreach email templates for prospective academic PIs and commercial CRO laboratory managers.

## Template 1: Academic Outreach — Enzyme Kinetics (Prof. Sharma, IIT Bombay)

**Subject**: Collaborative Research Inquiry: Carbonic Anhydrase Kinetics for Carbon Capture (CBMS Technologies)

Dear Prof. Sharma,

I hope this email finds you well.

I am writing to you on behalf of CBMS Technologies Pvt. Ltd., an industrial hardtech startup focused on biomimetic, coral-inspired mineral carbonation systems for flue gas capture. We have been following your lab’s outstanding work on carbonic anhydrase (CA) characterization and bioprocess optimization.

We are currently preparing a bench-scale experimental campaign to calibrate our reaction kinetics models (specifically target parameters \(k_{cat}\), \(K_M^{CO_2}\), and \(K_i^{HCO_3}\) under industrial operating conditions of 40°C and pH 8.5). We are particularly interested in characterizing wild-type bovine CA and a novel thermostable variant.

We believe your lab's biotech capabilities (specifically your high-throughput bioreactors, HPLC, and UV-Vis spectrophotometers) align perfectly with our experimental design. We would like to explore a collaborative partnership where CBMS funds a dedicated testing package (budgeted at ₹2,00,000 for consumables and student stipend top-ups) to carry out these runs in your lab.

Would you be available for a brief, 15-minute Zoom call next week (Tuesday or Wednesday afternoon) to discuss this potential collaboration? I have attached a brief abstract of our experimental protocol (CE-1) for your reference.

Thank you for your time, and I look forward to hearing from you.

Sincerely,

## Template 3: Commercial CRO Outreach — Sorption Isotherms (SGS India)

**Subject**: Request for Quote: High-Throughput Heavy Metal Sorption Analysis (Batch/Kinetic)

Dear SGS India Laboratory Team,

I am writing to request a formal price quote and feasibility assessment for a high-throughput heavy metal adsorption testing campaign on biopolymer (chitosan) substrates.

The required analytical specifications are as follows:
*   **Analytes**: Lead (Pb), Cadmium (Cd), Mercury (Hg), and Arsenic (As).
*   **Methodology**: Batch equilibrium tests (50 mL centrifuge tubes) and time-course kinetics.
*   **Analytical Instruments**: ICP-MS for Pb and Cd; Cold Vapor AAS for Hg; Hydride Generation AAS for As.
*   **Sample Load**: Approximately 350-400 liquid samples across varying concentrations (0.1 ppm to 100 ppm) requiring filtering (0.22 μm) and nitric acid stabilization.
*   **Turnaround Time**: We require a target completion window of 4-6 weeks from sample receipt.

Could you please provide:
1.  A standard rate sheet or project-level quote for the above services.
2.  Your typical sample intake requirements.
3.  Confirmation of your detection limits for each target metal in buffered saline solutions.

We look forward to receiving your quotation.

Best regards,

[Your Name]  
Procurement & Project Lead, CBMS Technologies Pvt. Ltd.  
[Contact Information]
