## 6. Search strings to self-source the remaining ~50 contacts

Run these directly in LinkedIn (basic search works, Sales Navigator is faster) and OpenVC's filter UI. Each typically surfaces 15–40 relevant profiles you can screen in minutes:

- `"carbon capture" AND India AND (investor OR angel OR "venture partner")`
- `"climate tech" AND "seed fund" AND Bangalore OR Mumbai`
- `CCUS AND India AND (professor OR faculty OR "principal investigator")`
- `"deep tech" AND India AND (mentor OR advisor) AND (chemical engineering OR materials science)`
- `site:linkedin.com/in "Indian Angel Network" AND climate`
- On OpenVC: filter Sector = Climate Tech + Deep Tech, Geography = India, Stage = Pre-seed/Seed — export the list directly
- On Climatescape: filter Technology = Carbon Capture and Utilization (CCU), Region = Asia

This method will get you past 100 within an hour, and — importantly — every name will be real, current, and checkable before you send a message.
