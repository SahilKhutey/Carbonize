PROBLEM ANALYSIS

The Industrial Decarbonization Trilemma

Traditional Carbon Capture and Storage (CCS) systems face unique constraints in emerging industrial belts.

bolt

The Energy Penalty

Traditional liquid solvent recovery loops (e.g. Monoethanolamine) demand immense heat for regeneration, imposing a parasitic thermal load of $3.0\text{--}4.2\text{ GJ/ton }\text{CO}_2$ captured.

grain

The High-Ash Barrier

Indian coal exhibits high ash concentrations ($35\text{--}45\%$). Traditional post-combustion column systems and delicate physical sorbents quickly plug or degrade under heavy particulate loading.

terrain

Geological Storage Cap

With few tested geological storage formations, high seismic risks, and expensive shipping networks, gaseous underground sequestration is impractical for most mid-sized utilities.
